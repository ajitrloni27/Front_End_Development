Description of Planets in solar system

Mercury
The smallest planet and closest to the Sun, known for its extreme temperature variations.
Venus
Often called Earth's "sister planet" due to its similar size, but it has a dense, toxic atmosphere and scorching surface temperatures
Earth
Our home planet, unique for its abundant liquid water and diverse life forms
Mars
The "Red Planet, known for its reddish hue and polar ice caps, a subject of intense exploration for potential past or present life.
Jupiter
The largest planet in our solar system, a gas giant with a prominent Great Red Spot, a massive storm. It has a vast system of moons.
Saturn
Famous for its stunning and intricate system of rings, composed of ice and rock particles. It is also a gas giant with many moons.
